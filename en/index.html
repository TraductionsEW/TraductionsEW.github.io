Translation

Keeping the human element in your texts

If you spend time and effort crafting copy that sets you apart, it’s important that your translator spend equal time and effort rendering the text for a new audience. For this reason, it’s important to choose someone with strong skills as a writer in their native language, and extensive cultural knowledge of the language communities they work with. With nearly ten years’ experience translating texts in various registers for clients in a range of domains from marketing to literature, and a background that includes working as a writer, I have a proven ability to clearly express my clients’ ideas in the right register and tone. My translations are also double checked by a second experienced translator for quality assurance.

Proofreading and Revision

Perfecting your content

Do you have a text in English that you need proofread or a translation that you need double-checked for accuracy and style? I do that too!

Rates

Rates for translation are usually billed per word of the source (original) document, which is standard practice in the industry. Proofreading, revision and more creative projects, including the translation of slogans or taglines, are billed per hour. Send me your text for a quote!
